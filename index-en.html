Welcome to Narcotics Anonymous - Amman

We are a fellowship of recovering addicts helping each other stay clean one day at a time.

Our Services Include:

  • Daily recovery meetings
  • 24/7 support
  • 12-Step program
  • Support groups in Arabic and English

We Help With Recovery From:

  • Tramadol and opioid addiction
  • Captagon and stimulant addiction
  • Lyrica and prescription drug addiction
  • Alcohol addiction
  • Heroin and other drug addiction
